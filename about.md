## About SoftNove

**SoftNove** is a digital solutions agency dedicated to building modern, scalable, and business-focused software products. We help startups, entrepreneurs, and organizations turn their ideas into reliable digital systems that grow with their business.

We believe technology should solve real problems—not create new ones. That’s why we focus on clean architecture, thoughtful design, and long-term sustainability in every project we deliver.

### What We Do

We specialize in:

- Custom website and web application development
- Mobile app development (Android, iOS, cross-platform)
- E-commerce and subscription-based platforms
- Backend, API, and system architecture
- EdTech, journal, portfolio, and business platforms
- Ready-made templates and reusable digital solutions
